# Data-Analysis-Project-Retail-Busisness-Insights

#📊 Tata Data Visualization Virtual Internship (Power BI)

This project is part of the Tata virtual internship program, where I performed data cleaning, analysis, and visualization to derive business insights from a retail dataset.

#🎯 Objective

The goal of this project was to analyze transactional retail data and present meaningful insights to business stakeholders (CEO & CMO) using interactive dashboards.

#🛠️ Tools Used
-Power BI
-Excel / CSV dataset

#🧹 Data Cleaning
Before analysis, the dataset was cleaned to ensure accuracy:

  -Removed records with negative or zero quantity (returns or invalid data)
  -Removed records with unit price ≤ 0

  -Created a Revenue column:
      (Revenue = Quantity × UnitPrice)
  -Converted date fields into proper format

#📊 Key Visualizations
1. Monthly Revenue Trend (2011)
-Identified seasonal patterns
-Revenue peaks during year-end (holiday season)
2. Top 10 Countries by Revenue (Excluding UK)
-Highlighted high-performing international markets
-Compared revenue with quantity sold
3. Top 10 Customers by Revenue
-Found that a small number of customers contribute a large portion of revenue
-Useful for customer retention strategies
4. Global Demand Analysis
-Identified countries with highest product demand
-Helps in expansion and market targeting decisions

#💡 Key Insights
-Strong seasonal sales trend in Q4
-Certain countries generate high revenue with lower volume
-Business follows Pareto principle (80/20 rule) in customer revenue
-High-demand regions present growth opportunities
